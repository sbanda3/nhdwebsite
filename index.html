NATIONAL HISTORY DAY CLUB

​Welcome to the National History Day (NHD) Club at WW-P High School South!

The NHD club is a research community dedicated to learning about history and creating awesome independent research projects, both for the National History Day competition and for fun! We strive to cultivate a supportive and collaborative environment where researchers ​can actively share ideas and feedback.
